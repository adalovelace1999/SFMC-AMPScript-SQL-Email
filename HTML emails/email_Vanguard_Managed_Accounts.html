You've worked hard, saved, and invested in your future.

So, what's next?

When it's time to start planning how to transition to retirement, we want you to do it with confidence. Yet, understanding how years of saving and investing translates into retirement income isn't always easy.

We can help

The Vanguard Managed Account Program offers additional features designed to help you be better prepared for and during retirement, including:

A personalized Income Plan that combines your retirement income sources to help you address any gaps or shortfalls, including Social Security guidance1 to help you maximize your lifetime retirement benefit

At your request, account rebalancing for growth and safety as you approach retirement to help limit market risk, without eliminating growth potential2

The opportunity for steady, long-lasting income with monthly payouts in retirement that could increase over time and last for life2

These additional features are included as part of the Vanguard Managed Account Program fees.3 Plus, you can receive ongoing support from Vanguard's team of managed account specialists who are available to answer your questions and provide you help when you need it.
